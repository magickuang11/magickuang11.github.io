Jie Zhu

Ph.D. in Computer Science

Short Bio

I have received my Ph.D. from Nanjing University in 2019.
Before that, I got my B.Eng degrees from Jiangnan University in 2012.
I have visited the University of Hong Kong during my PhD study, advised by Prof. Wenping Wang.

My research interest is Computer Graphics and Computer Vision.
